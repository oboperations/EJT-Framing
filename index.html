What We Do

Framing for Any Project, Any Scale.

From single-family homes to large commercial builds, EJT Framing Inc. handles every project with the same precision. We frame walls, floors, roofs, doors, windows, and full structural systems for residential and commercial clients across Jacksonville and Northeast Florida.

home

Residential Framing

From custom homes to multi-unit builds, we deliver precise framing that gives every residence a structural foundation built to last decades. Framed to code, on schedule, without shortcuts.

Commercial Framing

Large-scale commercial framing requires a crew that reads plans accurately and works to a tight schedule. We handle structural framing on commercial projects from the ground up with the reliability your GC needs.

ZIP System Installation

We professionally install ZIP System sheathing and tape, creating a continuous air and moisture barrier that outperforms traditional housewrap. Better protection starts at the framing stage, not after.

Walls, Floors & Roofs

Complete framing systems built to code and engineered to stand through anything Florida's climate delivers. We frame the full envelope, not just part of it.

Structural Framing

Full structural framing for any build type, designed for load-bearing strength from the ground up. We work directly from blueprints and build exactly to spec so inspections go smoothly.

construction

New Construction Framing

We frame new builds from dirt to dried-in, handling every structural element with speed and accuracy. Early-stage precision on new construction keeps every downstream trade on schedule.
